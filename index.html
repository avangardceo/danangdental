Common questions

Everything international patients ask before booking

Eight questions we hear most often from foreigners visiting Da Nang.

Do you speak English fluently at the clinic? +

Dr. Diep and her team speak fluent English. Diagnosis, treatment options and costs are explained in plain terms before any work begins. Most of our international patients book because of what they heard from another international patient.

How much does dental work in Da Nang cost? +

Cleaning starts from 200,000 ₫ (about 8 USD). Cosmetic and restorative work is quoted transparently during the free first consultation. No hidden fees, no surprise add-ons.

Where exactly is the dental clinic located? +

78 Phan Anh Street, Cẩm Lệ district, Da Nang. About ten minutes by taxi or Grab from the Han River bridges. Easy on-street parking outside.

Is dental treatment in Vietnam safe for foreign patients? +

Yes. The clinic uses international-standard materials, sterilization protocols and modern equipment — digital X-ray, 3D intraoral scanner, operating microscope, dental laser and computerized anesthesia. Dr. Diep completed advanced dental training in Canada.

How do I book an appointment with the dentist? +

WhatsApp is fastest at +84 905 151 522, especially in English. Zalo, phone, email and the form on this page all work. Appointments are typically confirmed within 24 hours.

What if I need urgent dental care during my trip to Da Nang? +

Same-day slots are available during opening hours when possible. For severe pain outside hours, message us on WhatsApp — Dr. Diep responds personally when she can. Please allow some time.

What payment methods does the clinic accept? +

Cash in Vietnamese đồng or US dollars, and bank transfer. Larger treatment plans can usually be split across visits.

How many visits does cosmetic dentistry typically require? +

Cleaning and fillings are usually one visit. Veneers, implants and full smile restorations are planned across two to four visits depending on the case. The full timeline is explained before treatment begins.
